The events reports for USA were retrieved from the NOAA's Storm Event Database at the following link:
https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/pub/data/swdi/stormevents/csvfiles

- Reports downloaded to compute the climatologies of rainfall events associated with flash flood events: 
      - from "StormEvents_details-ftp_v1.0_d2000_[mod_date].csv" to "StormEvents_details-ftp_v1.0_d2019_[mod_date].csv".

- Reports downloaded to compute the verification analysis: 
       - StormEvents_details-ftp_v1.0_d2022_[mod_date].csv.gz

The database was accessed on 18/03/2023.
